Assistants people **talk to** — conversational and **reactive**: a person drives, the assistant responds. The goal is one that's **accurate on a specific domain**, can **act** when asked, and stays reliable in a live chat.

- **Grounded in real knowledge (RAG).** Answers pulled from a specific knowledge base, with citations — accurate on your domain instead of generic or hallucinated.
- **Tool / function calling.** Calls functions to **fetch live data or trigger actions**, so a chat can actually *do* things, not just answer.
- **Built for live conversation.** **Streaming** replies, **memory** across turns, and an **embeddable widget** that drops into a site or app.
- **Reliable & flexible.** **Guardrails** (scope, safety, human hand-off) keep it trustworthy, and it's **multi-provider** (OpenAI / Claude) rather than locked to one model.
